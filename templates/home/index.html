About us

Welcome to Aksar Kounselling, a counselling services website dedicated to providing support and guidance to individuals from all walks of life. We believe that counselling is a valuable resource for anyone facing temporary challenges or dealing with long-standing issues. At Aksar Kounselling, we understand the impact of difficult childhood experiences and the feeling of being let down by parents, peers, or loved ones. We recognize the weight that perceived "failures" can carry and offer a safe space for you to navigate through these challenges. Our compassionate team is here to support you when you feel that love has slipped away, leaving behind feelings of loneliness. We understand the guilt that can come from thinking you're not doing enough and provide a non-judgmental environment for you to find healing and understanding. We embrace individuals who have made decisions that may not have met their expectations and offer a supportive space for those who have hidden their true sexual orientation due to societal pressures. At Aksar Kounselling, we foster acceptance and provide guidance to help you on your journey of self-discovery. For individuals with mental illness, we aim to challenge misconceptions and promote understanding of your unique experiences. Our experienced counselors specialize in addressing anxiety, depression, sadness, loneliness, hopelessness, self-esteem issues, and lack of confidence. We also recognize the importance of healthy relationships and family dynamics. Our marriage counselling services are designed to help couples communicate effectively, resolve conflicts, and regain a sense of connection. We address parenting disagreements, financial or lifestyle choices, emotional availability, loss of intimacy, physical appearance insecurities, and unequal distribution of household responsibilities. If you're struggling with substance abuse and mental health issues, Aksar Kounselling offers comprehensive support to address these interconnected challenges. Our counselors are dedicated to guiding you on the path to recovery and healing. At Aksar Kounselling, we are committed to providing a safe, confidential, and inclusive space for individuals from all backgrounds. Our goal is to empower you to overcome obstacles, achieve personal growth, and live a more fulfilling life. Take the first step towards a brighter future by reaching out to us and scheduling an appointment. Together, we can navigate life's complexities and help you embrace your true potential.

Services

At Aksar Kounselling, we offer a range of counselling services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Healing from Childhood Trauma

Our experienced counselors understand the impact of difficult childhood experiences and the challenges they can bring. We provide a safe and supportive space for individuals to navigate through their trauma and find healing. Our therapists specialize in trauma-focused therapy techniques to help clients process their past and develop coping strategies for a healthier future.

Student Counselling

At Aksar Counselling, we understand students' challenges: stress, anxiety, depression, anger issues. Our support fosters resilience. Overcome frustrations, build confidence, forge friendships. Embrace a brighter future. You're not alone; we're here for you. Visit Aksar Counselling and thrive!

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Counselling

We understand the interconnected challenges of substance abuse and mental health issues. Our comprehensive support aims to address both aspects of these struggles. Our counselors specialize in dual diagnosis treatment, helping individuals develop coping mechanisms, explore underlying issues, and establish a solid foundation for recovery. We provide personalized counselling sessions and create individualized treatment plans to support each client's unique journey.

Family Counselling

At Aksar Kounselling, we do offer family counselling services. Through family counselling, we provide a safe and supportive space for each family member to express their thoughts and feelings. Our therapists facilitate open and honest communication, helping family members gain a deeper understanding of each other's perspectives and find constructive ways to address conflicts or challenges.

Individual Counselling

We provide individual counselling services for those facing various personal challenges such as anxiety, depression, sadness, loneliness, hopelessness, self-esteem issues, lack of confidence, and other mental health concerns. Our counselors work collaboratively with individuals to explore their emotions, develop coping strategies, and promote personal growth and well-being.

Couples Counselling

Our couples counselling services are designed to support couples in navigating relationship difficulties and improving their communication and connection. We address common issues such as conflict resolution, trust-building, emotional intimacy, and strengthening overall relationship satisfaction. Our therapists provide a neutral and supportive space for couples to work through challenges and rediscover their bond.

Career Counselling

Our career counselling services aim to help individuals explore career options, identify their strengths and interests, and make informed decisions about their professional paths. Our career counselors provide guidance in areas such as career planning, job search strategies, resume building, interview preparation, and navigating career transitions. Additionally, we offer specialized counseling for further academic pursuits, assisting individuals in the process of seeking admissions both in the country and abroad.

Other Counselling

Our approach is founded on understanding and empathy, ensuring a safe space for you to explore your thoughts and feelings. We offer diverse counseling specialties, including personal growth, relationship issues, stress management, and more. Our qualified counselors are adept at addressing various life challenges, providing a supportive and non-judgmental environment. Through evidence-based techniques and compassionate guidance, we aim to empower you to overcome obstacles, develop coping strategies, and enhance overall well-being.
